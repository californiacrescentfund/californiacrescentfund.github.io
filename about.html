In recent years, Southern California has become a formidable hub for startups. However, while Silicon Beach is on the rise, VC presence here is not as saturated as Silicon Valley or the east coast. And while there are plenty of student-run VCs, Southern California has been mostly ignored. Crescent hopes to change this by tapping into the entrepreneurial talent at top local universities.
